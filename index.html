About Us

Dugout Indoor Cricket and Academy is Nepal’s premier indoor cricket facility, offering a modern, safe, and immersive environment for cricket enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. Our mission is to nurture talent, promote sportsmanship, and provide year-round access to high-quality cricket training and competitive matches.

With state-of-the-art pitches, professional coaching, and exciting events, Dugout Indoor Cricket and Academy ensures that players not only improve their skills but also enjoy the game in a vibrant and supportive community. Join us for training, leagues, and tournaments designed to inspire and challenge every player.

Our Services

Professional Coaching

Train with certified coaches who specialize in developing players of all skill levels. Our coaching programs focus on technique, strategy, fitness, and mental toughness, ensuring steady improvement and personal growth.

Indoor Matches

Enjoy playing competitive matches in our modern indoor courts. Our facilities allow year-round practice and match experience, with proper lighting, safety measures, and high-quality pitches that simulate real-game conditions.

Events & Tournaments

Participate in exciting tournaments and cricket events organized regularly for all age groups. We host leagues, friendly matches, and special workshops that provide opportunities to compete, learn, and network with fellow cricket enthusiasts.
